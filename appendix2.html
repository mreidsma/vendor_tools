Text Editors

To work with JavaScript files, you'll need a text editor (Word won't work!). There is a text editor already on your computer, but I suggest you look into one of the free text editors listed below that are designed for writing code:

Windows

  • Notepad : Built-in to Windows
  • Notepad++. Free and easy to use.
  • Atom: Free and very customizable.

Mac OSX

  • TextEdit.app : Built-in to Mac OS X
  • TextWrangler: Free and easy-to-use.
  • Atom: Free and very customizable.
  • SublimeText: A professional-grade text editor with a price tag to match. ($70). This is what I use, but a lot of folks have switched to Atom.

    • Linux

    • Atom: Free and very customizable.
